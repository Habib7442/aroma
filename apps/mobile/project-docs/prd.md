# AROMA Cafe & Restaurant — Product Requirement Document (PRD)

This document is the high-level Product Requirement Document for AROMA, a multi-outlet restaurant mobile application.

## Core Objective

Provide a unified, secure mobile experience for customers of three restaurant outlets in Silchar, Assam:
1. **AROMA** (Main Outlet — Indian, Chinese, and Tandoor)
2. **Cozy Cup** (Cafe & Bakery)
3. **Biryani Shop** (Specialty Biryani)
